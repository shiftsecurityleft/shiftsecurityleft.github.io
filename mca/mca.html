Managed Cloud Auditor


Managed Cloud Auditor monitors the configuration of resources within your AWS account to reduce the risk of configuration errors which may expose your sensitive data or leave your AWS resources vulnerable.
Select from an existing library of opinionated policies which represent the latest security controls for regulatory standards such as NIST SP800 or implement your own custom policies to represent your department, corporate, or agency requirements.
Automated notifications of policy violations are delivered via customer controlled web-hooks making it simple to integrate into existing issue tracking, messaging, and GRC systems.

Transparent - Automate monitoring of your AWS resources for potentially dangerous configuration errors.

Agile - Select from an existing library of vetted policies which cover best practices, NIST SP800 and several other regulatory standards.

Repeatable - Define and customize policies to address specific department, corporate, or agency requirements.

Secure - Enforcement of polices across your SDLC as both preventative and detective controls to help make security everyone's responsibility
